LIfe Shotz is located in the lakeside city of Coeur d’Alene, Idaho. Just 30 minutes east of Spokane, Washington, the northern Idaho Panhandle is an outdoor-lovers haven of spectacular natural beauty.

Fort Coeur d’Alene was constructed in the 1870s at the instruction of General William T. Sherman. The city that grew up around it now boasts a population approaching 50,000. Coeur d’Alene continues to expand as people flock here for its promise of fresh air and abundant year-round activities.
